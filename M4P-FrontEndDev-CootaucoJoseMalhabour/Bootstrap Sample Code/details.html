Dom Perignon

Dom Pérignon 2013 is an elegant, high-energy vintage champagne characterized by a "late harvest" profile, featuring a balance of creamy texture, vibrant acidity, and bright citrus notes. It offers a complex bouquet of white flowers, apricot, mint, and mineral ash. The palate is refined and silky, leading to a long, saline, and slightly smoky finish.

Château Giscours

Château Giscours 2022 is a highly acclaimed 3e Cru Classé from Margaux, recognized as a top-tier Bordeaux with a 13.5% ABV. It is a blend of 64% Cabernet Sauvignon, 30% Merlot, 3% Petit Verdot, and 3% Cabernet Franc. The wine offers a seductive, full-bodied profile featuring intense aromas of blackcurrants, cherries, violets, and forest floor, with silky, fine-grained tannins.

Aubert

The 2023 Aubert Chardonnays are lauded as some of Mark Aubert's best, benefiting from a cooler, longer growing season that produced high-energy, vibrant, and deeply complex wines. These Sonoma Coast and Carneros bottlings feature intense, layered flavors of apricot, hazelnut, lemon curd, and white peach, balanced by signature sharp acidity and polished, full-bodied textures.
